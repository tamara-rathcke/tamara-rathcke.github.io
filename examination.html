I am one of the departmental examiners for Konstanz students graduating with a teaching qualification in English Language and Linguistics. I examine on phonetics, phonology, morphology, sociolinguistics, varieties of English and psycholinguistics. Please contact me if you want to discuss your examination topics and readings.
